## Step 1: Access the App
- Open [https://yourdomain.com](https://yourdomain.com) in your browser.

## Step 2: Shorten Your First Link
1. Paste your long URL into the input field.
2. (Optional) Add a custom alias, e.g. `my-brand`.
3. Click **Shorten**.

## Step 3: Manage Your Links
- Log in to your account.
- Access the dashboard.
- Edit, copy, or delete your shortened links.
