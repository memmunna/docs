## Registration
---
Users can register with email and password by accepting terms and conditions, receiving a verification link via email, or sign up instantly using Google/Facebook without email verification.

**Screenshot**
![Registration Form](/images/authentication/registration.png)

## Login
---
Users can log in with valid email and password, optionally select "Remember Me," and upon success are redirected to the dashboard.

**Screenshot**
![Login Form](/images/authentication/login.png)

## Forgot Password
---
Users can reset their password by clicking `Forgot Password` on the login page, entering their registered email, and setting a new password via the reset link sent to their email.

**Screenshot**
![Forgot Password Form](/images/authentication/forgot-password.png)
